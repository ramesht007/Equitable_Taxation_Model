# Dataset Description 


The dataset was created as a part of survey by The Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) that conducts the Family Income and Expenditure Survey (FIES) nationwide. The survey, which is undertaken every three (3) years, is aimed at providing data on family income and expenditure, including, among others, levels of consumption by item of expenditure, sources of income in cash, and related information affecting income and expenditure levels and patterns in the Philippines.

The dataset was taken from this [link](https://www.kaggle.com/grosvenpaul/family-income-and-expenditure).
